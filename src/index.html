About company

By the decree No. 91 of the President of the Republic of Belarus joint close company BELINSOFT has the rights of a special exporter in the field of foreign trade, mediation for technical, cryptographic protection of information, means of undercover information obtaining related to specific goods (works, services).

The advantage of JCC «BELINSOFT» is to create individual integrated «turn-key» solutions in the field of national and public security.
