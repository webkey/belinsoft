About company

By the decree No. 91 of the President of the Republic of Belarus joint close company BELINSOFT has the rights of a special exporter in the field of foreign trade, mediation for technical, cryptographic protection of information, means of undercover information obtaining related to specific goods (works, services). The main export products of the company are tactical and strategic data interception systems, cybersecurity products, security systems for critical national infrastructure.

The advantage of JCC «BELINSOFT» is to create individual integrated “turn-key” solutions in the field of national and public security.

The company's products are a new direction of military-technical cooperation, development and integration of high-end technologies in special areas of activity, organization of experimental design and research works.
